# 🍋 Lemonade Stand Game Instructions

Welcome to the **Lemonade Stand Game**, where you're the entrepreneur! Manage your lemonade stand, make smart decisions, and see how much profit you can earn.

---

## 📋 Getting Started

1. **Enter your Player Name** and **Company Name**.
2. **Choose a Starting Budget**:
   - 🟢 Easy: $100
   - 🟡 Medium: $75
   - 🔴 Hard: $50
3. Click **Start Game** to begin.

---

## 🌤️ Morning Briefing

Every new day begins with important information:

- **Weather forecast**
- **Market prices** for:
  - 🍋 Lemons
  - 🧂 Sugar
  - 🥤 Cups
- **News headline** that may affect customer demand
- **Random event** (may boost demand)
- **Inventory report**

Click **Continue** when you're ready to proceed.

---

## 🧠 Make Business Decisions

Plan your day by making the following choices:

### 1. 💵 Set the Price per Cup
- Range: $0.25 to $5.00

### 2. 🥤 Decide How Many Cups to Make

### 3. 📦 Purchase Ingredients
- Lemons
- Sugar
- Cups

Prices change daily!

### 4. 📢 Select a Marketing Strategy
- **None ($0)** – no change to demand
- **Light ($10)** – small boost
- **Aggressive ($25)** – large boost

> 💡 You must also pay a fixed **Rent of $5** daily.

Watch the **Total Cost Estimate** to avoid overspending!

Click **Sell Lemonade** when ready.

---

## 📈 End-of-Day Sales Summary

After each day, you'll see:

- 🌤️ Weather
- 👥 Customer demand
- 🥤 Cups sold
- 💵 Revenue
- 💸 Expenses
- 📈 Profit
- 🏦 New Balance

Click **Next Day** to continue playing.

---

## 📊 Weekly Performance

At the end of each week (every 7 days), you'll get visual charts showing your:

- Profits
- Revenue
- Expenses

---

## ❌ Game Over

If your balance falls below $0.00, the game ends.  
💀 Better luck next time!

---

## 💡 Strategy Tips

- Hot days = more customers!
- Low prices may increase demand, but reduce profit per cup.
- Buying too many ingredients wastes money.
- Use marketing wisely — it’s expensive but powerful.
- Pay attention to news and events for hidden boosts.

---

Enjoy the game and build your lemonade empire! 🧃👑
